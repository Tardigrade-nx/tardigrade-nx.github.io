Game&Watch Fantasies

A homebrew game for PSP.

Download: GWF.zip / GWFv2.zip

Source: GWFv2_src.zip

Game&Watch Fantasies is a puzzle/platform game for the PSP, inspired by the Game&Watch LCD games.

  • Rule #1: Just like in a Game&Watch, the hero moves discontinuously. Therefore, he can “teleport” through thin obstacles by simply walking or jumping. However, gravity doesn’t allow him to do so: it is impossible to go through an obstacle when falling.

  • Rule #2: If the obstacle is too large, the hero will move until he touches it. This property can be used to adjust his horizontal or vertical position, depending on the situation.

Credits:
