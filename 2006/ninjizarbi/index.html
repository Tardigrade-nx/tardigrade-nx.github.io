[Nintendo DS] Ninji & Zarbi

Ninji & Zarbi

A homebrew game for the Nintendo DS.

Download: Ninji_nds.zip / Ninji_gba.zip / Ninji_sc.zip

Source: Ninji_0.1_src.zip

At any time, the two characters Ninji (bottom screen) and Zarbi (upper screen) are located at the same place on their respective screen. You control both characters at the same time.

The aim of the game is to achieve the highest score possible, by neutralizing Bullet Bills and catching the NDSes that appear on screen. Only Zarbi is able to catch the NDSes, as they always pop up on the upper screen. To neutralize a Bullet Bill, you have to stomp on its head like in Super Mario Bros. games, or attack it with a weapon. Only Ninji can handle weapons. Switch weapon with L/R.

Music: Cherry Blossom Girl by Air.
