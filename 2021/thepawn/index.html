[Android] The Pawn

The Pawn is an original puzzle game, inspired by chess. You don’t need to know the rules of Chess to play, as it is quite different.

  • Tap to move, one square at a time.
  • Move onto another friend piece to change into it.
  • Change into the king to win.
  • Move onto an enemy piece to knock it out.
  • Each piece has a different move set, indicated by arrows.
  • Slide on walls when moving diagonally.
